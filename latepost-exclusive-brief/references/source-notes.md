## Observed Form

- Title often begins with "晚点独家丨" or "独家丨", then names the actor and action.
- Lede prioritizes the new fact before background.
- Context explains why a personnel, financing, or product move signals strategic direction.
- Strong pieces connect small internal changes to larger market contests.
- Abstract is usually one understated sentence.

## Source Hygiene

- Treat "exclusive" as a sourcing status, not a style decoration.
- If the user lacks original reporting, remove exclusive labels and write as an analysis or reported brief.
