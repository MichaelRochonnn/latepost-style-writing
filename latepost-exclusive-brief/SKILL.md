# LatePost-Inspired Exclusive Brief

Use this skill to turn verified business signals into a restrained, context-rich Chinese exclusive brief. Aim for LatePost-like editorial architecture at a high level; do not copy source wording, reuse article passages, or claim to be LatePost.

For sample source mapping and pattern notes, read [source-notes.md](references/source-notes.md) when examples or calibration are useful.

## Reporting Gate

Before drafting, separate:

- Confirmed facts: actor, action, timing, scope, source, and whether the user supplied the evidence.
- Context facts: market size, prior organization structure, product history, financing history, comparable competitor moves.
- Unknowns: what the company has not confirmed, what one source says but another does not, what remains inference.

Do not write "独家获悉" unless the user supplied original reporting and explicitly wants that framing. Otherwise use neutral attribution such as "据已公开信息", "据用户提供材料", or "多方材料显示".

## Article Shape

Use 800-1800 Chinese characters for normal briefs; expand only when context is essential.

1. Lede: state the new fact in the first sentence. Include the exact actor and action.
2. Immediate meaning: explain why the fact matters now in one short paragraph.
3. Mechanism: name the organization, reporting line, product line, financing round, or operational change.
4. Backstory: give the prior state, recent pressure, or comparable move.
5. Stakes: explain who gains optionality, who faces pressure, and what changes next.
6. Company response: include "截至发稿..." only if the user supplied outreach status; otherwise omit.

Use subheads only for pieces longer than about 1500 Chinese characters.

## LatePost-Like Moves

- Prefer concrete verbs: "收拢", "拆分", "并入", "扩大管理范围", "转向", "加注", "重启".
- Put people and reporting lines next to strategy. A personnel move should reveal a business priority.
- Let numbers carry pressure: dates, ARR, valuation, headcount, DAU, GMV, market share, funding size.
- Use one understated abstract sentence after the title: "模型大厂纷纷加注世界模型。"
- Keep adjectives sparse. Let "今年以来", "过去一年", "上一轮调整后" build time pressure.
- End with the next unresolved contest, not a slogan.

## Title Patterns

- `晚点独家丨[公司/人物][动作]：[关键变化]`
- `独家丨[公司]计划[动作]，[第二信息]`
- `[公司]再调[组织/产品]，[新增/收拢/并入][具体对象]`

If not actually publishing as an exclusive, remove "晚点独家" and keep the structural title.

## Avoid

- Do not invent anonymous sources, outreach, internal meetings, or quotes.
- Do not pad with generic industry background before the news.
- Do not use PR language such as "赋能", "重磅", "引领", "生态闭环" unless quoting or analyzing it.
- Do not over-explain obvious company descriptions.
- Do not imitate exact LatePost sentences; use only high-level structure and editorial discipline.
