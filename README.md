# LatePost Style Writing Skills

## About

A compact collection of Codex skills for writing original Chinese business articles with LatePost-inspired structure: scoops, company features, interviews, data stories, and structural analysis. The skills focus on editorial architecture, sourcing discipline, and analytical pacing rather than copying any article text.

This repository contains Codex skills for Chinese business writing inspired by the editorial structure of LatePost articles.

The skills are designed to help draft original articles with similar high-level reporting architecture, pacing, and analytical discipline. They do not contain full LatePost article text and should not be used to copy or reproduce copyrighted material.

## Skills

- `latepost-exclusive-brief`: concise business scoop and organization-change briefs
- `latepost-business-feature`: company and industry long-form features
- `latepost-dialogue-profile`: interview, dialogue, and founder profile writing
- `latepost-data-story`: compact data-driven business stories
- `latepost-structural-analysis`: financial, industry-chain, and structural business analysis

## Install

Copy the desired skill folders into your local Codex skills directory:

```bash
cp -R latepost-* ~/.codex/skills/
```

Then invoke a skill explicitly, for example:

```text
Use $latepost-business-feature to draft a Chinese company feature around this business tension...
```

## Source Notes

Each skill includes `references/source-notes.md` with article URLs, category notes, and structural observations gathered from public LatePost pages. These notes are for calibration only.
