## Observed Form

- Title usually names the dataset or phenomenon and appends "丨晚点小数据".
- The story is short and chart-adjacent; it rarely tries to explain everything.
- A good ending reframes what the data implies about an era, cycle, or business model.

## Source Hygiene

Keep dataset limitations visible. Never infer causality without additional evidence.
